About Design

Design is an inherently creative and problem-solving process that combines art, science, and functionality to create products, environments, or experiences that serve a specific purpose. At its core, design is about making things not only aesthetically pleasing but also usable, functional, and effective in solving problems. It goes beyond mere decoration; design is an intentional process of transformation and innovation that blends form and function. Design can be seen as both an art form and a technical discipline. Whether it’s a logo, a building, a mobile app, or even a service, design shapes our world. It's a universal language that influences how we interact with our surroundings, how we communicate, and how we understand the world. It's a process that requires an understanding of human behavior, the environment, culture, and the constraints of materials or technology.
