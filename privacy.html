Privacy Policy

At Brainware, we take the privacy of our clients and visitors to our website very seriously. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you interact with our design services or visit our website. We understand that your privacy is important, and we are committed to ensuring that your data is handled with the utmost care and in compliance with all relevant privacy regulations. When you engage with our design services, whether it be through commissioning custom designs, submitting project details, or simply browsing our website, we may collect certain personal and project-related information, including your name, contact details, billing information, and project specifics. Additionally, we may collect usage data such as IP addresses, browser types, and device information when you visit our website. This data is used primarily to fulfill our design services, process payments, communicate effectively with you, and improve the overall customer experience. We only use your personal information for purposes directly related to your project or interactions with us, such as providing the design work requested, processing payments, providing customer support, or sending necessary updates regarding the project. We will not share your personal data with third parties for their own marketing purposes without your consent.

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, billing address, and payment details.
  • Design Services: To deliver the requested design work and communicate throughout the project
  • Billing and Payments: To process payments and manage invoices for completed work.
  • Customer Support: To respond to inquiries and provide support.
  • Improvement and Marketing: To enhance the services provided, analyze website traffic, and send updates or promotional materials
